FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it in original condition for a full refund or exchange.

How long does shipping take?

Domestic orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days. International shipping takes 7-14 business days. Free shipping on orders above ₹999.

Do you offer warranty on products?

Yes! All our products come with a minimum 1-year manufacturer warranty. Premium products have extended warranty up to 3 years.

Do you have a physical store?

Yes! Visit our GameZone Gaming Hub in Mumbai. You can test our products and get expert advice from our gaming specialists.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders, gaming cafes, and esports teams. Contact our sales team for customized quotes.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can also track your order in your account dashboard.
