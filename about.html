Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming India's premier gaming accessories brand

Born from Passion

GameZone was founded in 2018 by three professional gamers who were frustrated with the lack of high-quality, affordable gaming accessories in the Indian market. What started as a small online store selling imported gaming mice has grown into a comprehensive gaming ecosystem.

Our founders - Raj, Ankit, and Priya - competed in national eSports tournaments and understood firsthand the difference that quality gear makes in competitive gaming. They set out to create products that would give Indian gamers the same advantages as international competitors.

Today, GameZone partners with professional eSports teams and streamers to test and refine our products, ensuring they meet the highest standards of performance and durability.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the GameZone story

2018

GameZone is Born

Founded by three professional gamers with a vision to bring premium gaming accessories to the Indian market. Started as an online store with just 3 products.

2019

First Product Line Launch

Launched our first proprietary gaming mouse, designed specifically for competitive FPS gaming. Sold 5,000 units in the first 3 months.

2020

Esports Partnerships

Partnered with 5 professional esports teams as official gear providers. Expanded product line to include keyboards, headsets, and controllers.

2021

Retail Expansion

Opened first physical store in Mumbai. Launched in 15 retail locations across India. Reached 50,000 customers milestone.

2022

International Recognition

Won "Best Gaming Accessories Brand" at India Gaming Awards. Expanded shipping to 5 countries. Launched professional streaming equipment line.

2023

Innovation Lab Launch

Opened R&D center focused on gaming ergonomics and sensor technology. Launched patented "TrueTrack" sensor technology in our flagship mouse.
